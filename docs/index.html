How many YAMv2 will I get?

Your YAMv2 balance will be the your OG YAM balance.

YAM that you buy today can be converted to YAMv2 at the current ratio.

The current ratio will change with rebasing, but your OG balance will not.

There will be a maximum of 5,000,000 OG YAMs and 5,000,000 YAMv2.

What about voting?

If you were in the vote delegation snapshot, your vote balance was your OG YAM balance at the time that voting occurred.
